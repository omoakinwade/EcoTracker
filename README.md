# EcoTracker

A decentralized environmental action tracking platform that incentivizes sustainable behaviors and rewards consistent environmental activism through blockchain-based eco tokens.

## Overview

EcoTracker is a smart contract platform that enables environmental activists to track their eco-friendly actions and earn tokens based on their sustainability commitment. The system includes features for tracking sustainability levels, conserving tokens for long-term environmental goals, and managing a sustainable eco economy.

## Features

- **Action Tracking**: Record and validate environmental actions and sustainability efforts
- **Sustainability Bonuses**: Reward activists who maintain consistent eco-friendly behaviors
- **Token Redemption**: Allow activists to redeem earned eco tokens
- **Token Conservation**: Enable activists to conserve tokens for long-term environmental projects
- **Environmental Statistics**: Track overall action metrics and token distribution

## Core Functions

- `start-eco-action`: Begin tracking a new environmental action
- `complete-eco-action`: Finalize an action and receive eco tokens
- `claim-eco-rewards`: Exchange accumulated tokens for environmental benefits
- `conserve-eco-tokens`: Conserve tokens for long-term environmental commitments
- `release-conserved-tokens`: Retrieve conserved tokens (with potential penalties for early release)

## Technical Details

- Maximum eco impact capacity: 2,500,000 tokens
- Base action reward: 18 tokens
- Sustainability bonus: 6 tokens per level (up to 9 levels)
- Minimum conservation duration: 1008 blocks (approximately 7 days)
- Early release penalty: 18%

## Getting Started

1. Deploy the contract to your blockchain
2. Environmental activists can start tracking their eco actions
3. Complete actions to earn eco tokens
4. Conserve tokens for long-term environmental goals
5. Redeem tokens for environmental benefits when ready

## Security

The system includes safeguards to prevent exceeding the eco impact capacity and ensures that only legitimate environmental actions are rewarded.
